About This Tool

Created by NAZEER KADHAR, HSE Professional

HSE Heat Stress Alert System is a professional tool designed to monitor real-time heat stress risks for outdoor workers and construction sites.


Features:

  • Real-time heat index calculation
  • WBGT (Wet Bulb Globe Temperature) monitoring
  • Location-based weather data
  • WhatsApp alert subscriptions
  • Risk level assessments (Safe/Caution/Extreme)

Purpose: To prevent heat-related illnesses and ensure worker safety during extreme weather conditions.
